The Founding Chronicle

A Record of the First 72 Hours

Before the Compact, before the Guilds, there was only the struggle to survive. This chronicle details the coordinated plan that transformed a scattered group of survivors into the society of Valerock. Let it serve as a reminder of our humble beginnings and the power of cooperation.

Day 1: The First Steps

Morning: The Great Gathering

  • The Rally: All citizens converged on the town center, knapping a Stone Axe and Stone Knife.
  • The Founding Party: Under the direction of the Founding Warden, four critical task forces were formed:

Forager Force: Tasked with harvesting all edible berries, mushrooms, and wild vegetables.

Fuel & Log Force: Tasked with gathering dry sticks and chopping trees for essential firewood.

Clay & Stone Force: Tasked with collecting vast quantities of clay from the nearest water source.

Cattail Force: Tasked with gathering cattail rushes for baskets and roots for sustenance.

Afternoon: The Race Against the Sun

  • A battery of communal firepits was constructed and lit by the Camp Master.
  • The first communal meal of cooked roots and mushrooms was prepared and distributed.
  • Using dry grass, every citizen crafted a straw bed, establishing a safe, shared respawn point.

Evening: Securing the Night

  • A perimeter of torches was established to light the camp.
  • Synchronized Sleep: In the most critical act of coordination, all citizens slept at the same time, allowing the night to pass instantly and safely.

Day 2: Laying the Foundation

  • The Pottery Initiative: The Pottery Team began mass production with a strict priority: Bowls for all, then Cooking Pots, Crocks, and finally, Pickaxe Molds and Crucibles for the coming metal age.
  • The Basket Initiative: All citizens crafted a reed basket from the gathered cattails, immediately increasing personal inventory space and efficiency.
  • The First Structures: The proto-Builder's Guild began construction of a simple Larder and a communal Workshop.
  • All foraging parties were under a standing order to always save the seeds from wild crops.

Day 3: The Guilds Emerge

  • The Founding Party was formally dissolved.
  • The first fired pickaxe molds were distributed, and the Mining & Prospecting Guild was born, tasked with finding surface copper.
  • The Pottery Team became the core of the new Smithing & Crafting Guild, preparing to smelt the first ores.
  • A dedicated team formed the Lumber & Farming Guild, tilling the first farm plot and managing the vital firewood supply.
  • Explorers were formalized into the Hunter & Charter Guild, tasked with mapping the surrounding lands.
  • The builders were formally established as the Builder's Guild, continuing their work on communal structures.
  • By evening, the Founding Warden appointed the first five Wardens, the Valerock Compact was enacted, and the age of society had begun.
